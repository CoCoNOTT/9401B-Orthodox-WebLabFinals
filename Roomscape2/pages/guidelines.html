This consignee shall be responsible for the following
1. to answer for any damage/s incurred during the use of this facility and its premises.
2.to keep and maintain cleanliness, peace and order during and immediately after the activity

4. to prohibit the entry of persons carrying or possessing knife, gun, or any kind of deadly weapon within the place, and ot refuse the entry of any person who has no official business with the activity/affair throughout the duration of the approved use of the facility/place.
